Get Started with Donezo

Donezo lets you create a dead simple to-do list. Tasks automatically expire after 7 days if you don't complete them. This strategy is loosely based on the Pareto Principle, which (sort of) states that on any given to-do list, 80% of the tasks don't really need to be on there.

So, Donezo automatically archives the stuff that wasn't important enough for you to focus on in a week. The archived tasks are still available, under the Expired Tasks tab. You can reactivate them when you're ready for them. Sign up or log in below to get started.
